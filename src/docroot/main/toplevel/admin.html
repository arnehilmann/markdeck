Convert Slides into PDF

Disclaimer: Tested with Google Chrome on MacOS only right now.
  1. Click render pdf.
  2. Wait for a fresh browser window to open.
  3. Wait for the "Choose what to share" dialog.
  4. Select "Chrome Tab".
  5. Select "* * * MARKDECK PDF RENDERING * * *".
  6. Click "Share".
  7. Wait for the rendering to finish.
  8. The pdf can be found in the Download location of your browser.
Note: Keep the area of the new window clear! (Screenshots will be taken! Hide your mouse, too!)
